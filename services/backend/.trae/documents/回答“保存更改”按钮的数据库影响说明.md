## 后端接口（新增）
- 路由：`POST /api/react/v1/local-supplier-invoices/{invoiceGuid}/details/batch-upsert`
- 请求DTO：`InvoiceDetailUpsertItemDto[]`
  - 字段（与模型对应）：
    - `detailGUID?`（有则更新，无则创建）
    - `itemNumber?`, `barcode?`, `productName?`, `productCategoryGUID?`
    - `quantity?`, `lastPurchasePrice?`, `purchasePrice?`, `retailPrice?`, `amount?`
    - `discountRate?`, `autoPricing?`, `pricingFloatRate?`, `newAutoRetailPrice?`, `isSpecialProduct?`
- 服务实现：`LocalSupplierInvoicesReactService.BatchUpsertDetailsAsync(invoiceGuid, items)`
  - 事务包裹；按 `detailGUID` 查找更新；无 `detailGUID` 则插入并补齐 `InvoiceGUID/StoreCode/SupplierCode`
  - 更新审计字段 `UpdatedAt/UpdatedBy`；插入时填充 `CreatedAt/CreatedBy/IsDeleted=false`
